WE ARE UNITED

WE UNITE is an initiative of Kalki Foundation Fashion and Lifestyle a division of Reliance Industries PVT LTD is India's fastest growing branded e-commerce company with premium parters across many Indian based companies.It introduced premier international labels, enabling Indian consumers to buy the most prestigious global products ranging from fashionwear to electronics and accessories within the country.

We have all the products that you have ever thought of that too with Indian brand license and all our products are approved by the Department of National Products Of India.

Categories of products we deal in include: Electronics, Fashion(Men,Women,Chldren of all age groups), household, Groceries, Multimedia, Health Care, Medicine, Dairy Products, Foot-Wear and many more.

We are on a mission to make our country India manufacture and consume its own products across numerous sectors(Fashion,Electronics and media, sports, Health Care etc).We value our Indian poroducts and if all of you join us, we can together have a great impact on the economy of our country and that time is not far away when our country will again be known as the 'Golden Bird'!
